Investify

Date: 9th March 2025

Time: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Team Size: 2 to 3 members

Event Flow

Initial Investment

At the start of the event, each team will receive Rs. 100 as seed capital.

Utilization of Funds

  • Teams can use their Rs. 100 for purchasing goods, trading, or investing in services to generate profit.
  • Personal expenses (e.g., travel, food, etc.) are strictly prohibited.

General Rules

Time Limit

Teams will have 240 minutes (4 hours) to execute their investment strategies.

During this period, teams can operate outside the college campus to maximize their profits.

Monitoring & Guidance

Each team will be assigned a club representative to ensure adherence to the rules and provide necessary guidance.

Transaction Records

Teams must maintain a detailed record of all transactions, including:/p>

  • Amount spent
  • Items purchased
  • Any subsequent sales or earnings

Return & Profit Sharing

At the end of the event, teams must return:

  • The Rs. 100 initial investment
  • 30% of their total profit

Winning Criteria

The team with the highest net profit (after returning Rs. 100 and 30% profit share) will be declared the winner.
