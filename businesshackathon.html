Sparkulate: The Entrepreneurial Showdown

Date: 8th March 2025

Registration Fee: ₹150 per team

Team Formation

  • Team Size: 3-5 members
  • Total Teams: Subject to registration count

General Rules

  • Follow all instructions from event coordinators.
  • No cheating, external help, or unauthorized devices.
  • Strict time limits must be adhered to.
  • Elimination rounds determine progression.

Event Coordinators

  • Vaishnavi Ahire: +91 8530017432
  • Rutuja Gherde: +91 7620921259

Event Flow

Round 1: Menti Meter (Offline - 10:00 AM to 11:00 AM)

Format: Depending on registrations, this round may be eliminatory or preparatory.

Round 2: Debate Pitching (Offline - 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM)

Venue: Mechanical Conference Hall

Format: Competitive elimination round where teams with the same problem statement compete for an investor.

Example: Uber vs. Ola – each team must defend their business model.

Round 3: Final Business Hackathon (Offline)

Top 10 Teams are shortlisted for the final round

Duration: 2 hours 30 minutes + 50 minutes (5 minutes per team)

Format: Hackathon-style competition with mentor interaction and continuous evaluation.

Teams will develop solutions within the given timeframe and pitch to judges based on feasibility, innovation, and business impact.

Scoring & Elimination Rules

  • Performance in each round determines rankings.
  • Early elimination applies based on results.
  • Judges' decisions are final.
